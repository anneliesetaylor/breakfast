Tomato and Bacon Egg Muffins

Resembling mini-quiches, these egg muffins are the perfect quick breakfast dish to satisfy all kinds of savory palettes. Fast and easy to prepare, one batch of these can have breakfast covered for the entire week. And, their high protein content is sure to keep you full all morning.

Photo by Margo Brodowicz from Unsplash. Recipe adapted from Allrecipes.com.

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Yields: 6 servings

  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes cut in half
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 1 pinch of ground black pepper

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit. Spray 6 muffin cups with cooking spray.
  2. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until halfway cooked, about 5 minutes. Blot bacon slices with paper towels and cut into small pieces.
  3. Mix bacon, eggs, tomatoes, green onion, and black pepper together in a bowl. Pour mixture into the prepared muffin cups.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until each muffin is set in the middle, 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Serve fresh out of the oven, or store in the fridge and reheat in the microwave when ready to eat.
