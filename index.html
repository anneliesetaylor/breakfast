A Guide to: The Most Important Meal of the Day

Arguably the most underappreciated meal of the day, breakfast is the key to living a healthy and productive life. Aptly named, breakfast is the meal that breaks the overnight fasting period by replenishing your supply of glucose and, in turn, boosting your energy levels and alertness. The benefits of eating a good breakfast are endless, with some of the most notable being the meal's ability to improve your ability to concentrate, reduce risk of type 2 diabetes and heart disease, as well as improving your body's ability to manage your weight (according to Web MD).

And, while it is understandable that not everyone has time in the morning to sit down for a balanced breakfast, there is still no excuse for waiting until lunch to have your first meal of the day. Skipping breakfast can be detrimental to your body's rhythm of fasting and eating, and your body will be feeling those long-term effects long after lunchtime.

Some of the best ways to combat one's tendency to skip the most important meal of the day is to find recipes that get you out of your boring-breakfast-rut and preplan for the days when you know you'll be more inclined to skip the meal. And this website can help you do exactly that!
